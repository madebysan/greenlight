# Cast & Crew: Everything Everywhere All At Once

### EVELYN WANG
- **Description:** 55 year old Chinese woman in an oversized t-shirt, streaks of gray in her hair, and thin outdated designer glasses. Exhausted laundromat owner.
- **Arc:** Evolves from a fractured, disappointed woman into an omniversal being who chooses love, mundane reality, and her messy family over nihilism.
- **Scenes:** 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 25, 48, 56, 62, 67, 80, 85, 86, 90, 91, 94, 101, 107, 110, 118, 124
- **Special requirements:** Extensive martial arts choreography; Sign spinning; Opera singing; Hot dog finger prosthetics
- **Wardrobe changes:** 15

### WAYMOND WANG
- **Description:** Evelyn's husband in transition lens glasses and an ill fitting polo shirt. Sweet but seeking a divorce.
- **Arc:** Transforms from a seemingly weak husband into the moral anchor of the multiverse, teaching Evelyn that kindness is a survival strategy.
- **Scenes:** 1, 2, 4, 6, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 17, 48, 56, 67, 80, 85, 86, 101, 110, 118, 124
- **Special requirements:** Fanny pack martial arts choreography
- **Wardrobe changes:** 5

### ALPHA WAYMOND
- **Description:** A variant of Waymond from the Alphaverse. Confident, tactical, capable fighter.
- **Arc:** Searches the multiverse for an Evelyn who can defeat Jobu Tupaki, ultimately sacrificing himself for the cause.
- **Scenes:** 14, 16, 17, 23, 25, 86
- **Special requirements:** Fanny pack martial arts choreography; Stunts
- **Wardrobe changes:** 2

### JOY WANG
- **Description:** Evelyn's daughter, 20s, sporting a worn down hoodie and a stoic, blank expression.
- **Arc:** Struggles with her mother's lack of acceptance, seeking a reality where they can either understand each other or fade into nothingness together.
- **Scenes:** 1, 3, 4, 6, 80, 85, 118, 124
- **Special requirements:** None listed
- **Wardrobe changes:** 3

### JOBU TUPAKI
- **Description:** An omniversal chaos agent possessing Joy's body. Wildly changing, extravagant costumes.
- **Arc:** Seeks an Evelyn who can see the meaninglessness of the multiverse with her, hoping to be saved from the Bagel.
- **Scenes:** 18, 56, 67, 86, 90, 91, 94, 101, 110, 118
- **Special requirements:** Constant and rapid wardrobe and makeup changes; Stunts; Pig handler
- **Wardrobe changes:** 20

### GONG GONG
- **Description:** Evelyn's demanding elderly father, hunched over his cane, white undershirt and slippers.
- **Arc:** Remains deeply critical of Evelyn, but is ultimately defended by her despite his harsh judgments.
- **Scenes:** 2, 6, 12, 13, 14, 15, 16, 118, 124
- **Special requirements:** Wheelchair
- **Wardrobe changes:** 1

### ALPHA GONG GONG
- **Description:** Ruthless military commander of the Alphaverse forces, operating from a tricked-out wheelchair.
- **Arc:** Determined to assassinate Jobu and Evelyn to save the multiverse, regardless of the emotional cost.
- **Scenes:** 67, 85, 101, 110
- **Special requirements:** Makeshift office supply robot exoskeleton; Wheelchair stunts
- **Wardrobe changes:** 1

### DEIRDRE ATKINS
- **Description:** A frumpy IRS auditor in a cheap suit, carrying a hand grip strengthener.
- **Arc:** Goes from antagonistic bureaucrat to a victim of the multiverse, ultimately receiving empathy from Evelyn.
- **Scenes:** 15, 16, 17, 25, 48, 62, 107, 124
- **Special requirements:** Pro wrestling stunts; Hot dog finger prosthetics
- **Wardrobe changes:** 4

### DEIRDRE (BAGEL)
- **Description:** A zealot follower of Jobu Tupaki in the Temple Universe, adorned with a sharpie circle on her forehead.
- **Arc:** Serves as Jobu's ruthless lieutenant before being disarmed by Evelyn's radical empathy.
- **Scenes:** 18, 25, 56, 91, 107
- **Special requirements:** Stunts
- **Wardrobe changes:** 2

### BECKY SREGOR
- **Description:** Joy's girlfriend, tattoos, septum piercing.
- **Arc:** Supports Joy through her family struggles and is eventually accepted by Evelyn.
- **Scenes:** 3, 4, 6
- **Special requirements:** None listed
- **Wardrobe changes:** 1
