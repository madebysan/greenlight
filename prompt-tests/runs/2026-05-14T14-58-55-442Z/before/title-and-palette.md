## Palette Source

- **Bureaucratic Gray** `#8B8680` — The drained, fluorescent-tinged palette of the IRS building and apartment interiors, where most of the emotional core resides; the film's anchor in mundane, lived-in reality.

- **Soy Sauce Brown** `#3E2723` — The warm, slightly burnt brown of overcooked noodles and cluttered domestic spaces; specifically Evelyn's defensive food preparation and the textures of family shame.

- **Neon Magenta** `#E91E63` — The violent, impossible colors of the multiverse ruptures and alternate realities; signaling that the film's absurdity is not ethereal but visceral and occasionally nauseating.

- **Pale Institutional White** `#F5F5F5` — The white of the temple universe's massive veil, the light of Waymond's kindness, and the visual space where nihilism and redemption meet; a color of both emptiness and possibility.

- **Mud Green** `#556B2F` — The desaturated, almost sickly green of the rock universe and the film's quietest moments; a color that suggests exhaustion, groundedness, and the peace found in giving up.
